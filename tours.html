The Seguz 6 Day & 5 Nights Experience.

DAY 1: Durban Touch down at King Shaka international Airport (Durban) where you all be met and transferred to your hotel. Spend the rest of the day at leisure, exploring the magnificent beaches of Durban. At 18h00 we all meet up at the reception for a briefing on the tour and a chance to meet your fellow travelers OVERNIGHT: Accommodation: Elangeni Hotel or similar

DAY 2: Touring around Durban and transfer to Hluhluwe At 8H30 you will be transferred to Mabhida soccer stadium. The new icon of Durban, the impressive soccer stadium was built for the 2010 World Cup in Kwazulu Natal where you will catch a Sky car ride up to the stadium arch which takes Two-minute, before you step onto the platform and take in the unparalleled 360 view of Durban and beyond. The sky car gives you the chance to discover Durban from a 106m- high vantage point. Look one way and take in the ocean views as for as the eye can see. Of Corse if you are up for a bit of a workout, the you can walk up to the top of the arch taking in the scenery as you make your way up the 500 steps. It’s a leisurely 20-minute walk to the top, with breathtaking views to reward you. Seventy meters up, you will come across the Big Swing platform where the brave leap into the stadium bowl. You will also be taken to stadium tour for an 1h00 30m.the tour will give you full access to the state-of-the-art changing room, walk through the tunnels they walk through, step onto the pitch and imagine thousands of roaming fans around you. You can also opt to do the stadium tour by Segway. Then at about 12h00 you can have Lunch at one of the amazing restaurants in the stadium (at your cost).at 13h00 you will be transferred to the city of Durban for a City tour with the open bus. Where you will get to learn more about the history of the City of Durban. At 16h00 you will be transferred to Hluhluwe which will take about 3hours Overnight accommodation in Hluhluwe Hlulala Guest House or similar.

DAY 3: Game viewing at Hluhluwe Umfolozi Game reserve At 5h00 in the morning Board the 4*4 open game drive vehicle as we go on an early safari Game drive to Hluhluwe Umfolozi Game Reserve, witness the wildlife in the oldest proclaimed game reserve in Africa. Keep an eye out for the Big 5, African wild Dog and Cheetah that hunt in the open plains. During the game viewing you will have Traditional South African bush barbecue in the reserve while observing the wildlife at one of the many scenic picnic sports. With spectacular vantage points your personal and knowledgeable game ranger will get you the sighting that you love to see, this is a full day tour. Then you will go back to your accommodation.

DAY 4: Isimangaliso Wetland Park and Leopard Sanctuary 8h30 in the morning you will be transferred to Isimangaliso Wetland Park which will take about 1hour drive. Where you will get on the Boat cruise where you will travel up to one of the largest Estuarine systems in Africa as well as being the South Africa’s very first World Heritage Site. Home to 800 Hippos and approximately 1000 Nile Crocodiles, not to mention the abundance of Bird-life. The cruise takes about 2 hours, at 12h00 you will have lunch in one of the amazing sea food restaurant in St Lucia. After lunch at around 13h30 you will be taken to the beach, you can walk along the beautiful ocean for about an hour and half, at 14h30 you will be transferred back to Hluhluwe on our way back we will take a short left for an up close and personal tour and learn more about South Africa’s endangered wild cat species. You will be mesmerized by the beauty of the caracal (Lynx) enchanted by the playful Serval, astonish by the speed and ability of the Cheetah and delighted by the African Wildcat the tour takes about an hour. Then you will be transfer back your accommodation.in Hluhluwe.

DAY 5: Sodwana Bay AT 8h00 you will be transferred to Sodwana Bay where you will enjoy the marine reserve in the World Natural Heritage Site at Sodwana Bay where you have a choice of scuba and skin diving, walking or horse riding on the magnificent endless white beach and enjoying the warm ocean. You will have till 15h00 where you will be transferred back to your accommodation to relax till 18h00 where will all meet for a boma dinner for our last day of our trip

DAY 6: ShakaLand and King Shaka Air Port At 8h00 you will be transferred to ShakaLand to get a unique insight into the traditional culture and customs of the proud Zulu Nation kraal in Zululand, which include lunch. Sample traditional Zulu beer, marvel at the energy and rhythm of the Zulu hut, and appreciate the artistry of fashioning traditional weapons and crafts. Visit an iSangoma-traditional healer and 13h00 you will be transferred to King Shaka Airport where we will say good bye to you. End of the tour Download PDF format
